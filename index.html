The Cozy World of Ollie the Cat — Book 1

The Mighty Mouse-Cat

A cozy bedtime story about finding strength within

by Cherry Bloom

Ollie is a curious little cat with a big heart who dreams of becoming the strongest thing in the world. As he explores the world around him, he begins to wonder where true strength really comes from…

✨  Sometimes the greatest strength is simply believing in who you are.

A beautifully illustrated story that encourages confidence, self-belief, kindness, and emotional growth.

Perfect for

  • Bedtime reading
  • Animal lovers
  • Cozy family story time
  • Learning to believe in yourself

Ages 3 – 7
